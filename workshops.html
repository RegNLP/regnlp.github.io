RegNLP 2025
The First Workshop on Regulatory Natural Language Processing

Overview

RegNLP 2025 took place on January 20, 2025, co-located with the COLING 2025 conference in Abu Dhabi, UAE. The workshop brought together researchers and practitioners working at the intersection of NLP, law, and compliance to discuss methods, resources, and applications for regulatory text.
